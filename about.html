Praxis & Intention

This ledger is a consecrated space of nocturnal reflection, devotional observation, dream reclamation, and symbolic experimentation in dialogue with Inanna / Ishtar (the Morning & Evening Star), and the liminal currents associated with Lilith’s mythic shadow.

Approach

  • Ritual Cadence: Entries structured around phases (star-rise, invocation, descent, integration).
  • Symbolic Weaving: 8-pointed star (celestial sovereignty), pentagram (microcosmic alignment).
  • Dream Recollection: Recorded promptly; annotated with astral & emotional vectors.
  • Integration: Tags unify visionary, empirical, and somatic strands.

Disclaimers

No claim of historical orthodoxy: this is a living, syncretic, creative ritual journal. Respect living traditions & cultural lineages.
